Featured Composition

My latest work exploring the connection between botanical growth patterns and musical structure.

"Symbiosis: A Botanical Symphony"

This composition translates the growth patterns of mycorrhizal networks into melodic structures, creating an auditory representation of the complex relationships between fungi and plant roots. The piece evolves gradually, mirroring the slow, deliberate growth of underground networks that connect entire forests.

My Musical Approach

As both a biologist and musician, my work exists at the intersection of science and art. I use music as a medium to translate complex biological processes and patterns into auditory experiences that can be intuitively understood.

My compositions often begin with field recordings or data collected during research, which are then transformed into musical elements using various techniques including sonification, algorithmic composition, and traditional orchestration.

The goal of my musical work is not just artistic expression, but also to create new ways of understanding and connecting with natural phenomena that might otherwise remain abstract or invisible.
