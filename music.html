My Musical Approach

As both a biologist and musician, my perspective exists at the intersection of science and art. I use art and data interchangeably to bring clarity to my world, find beauty in that world, and contribute as much beauty as I can.

What inspires me is vast. I'm attracted to big ideas, big feelings that find their form in the smallest things. Whether it's the emergent qualities of bacteria in community, musicians at a jam, or the smallest parts of the production coming together to form the play, I love how small units coalesce to become something more beautiful than the sum of their parts.

The goal of my musical work is not just artistic expression, but a careful inspection of how we all make each other more beautiful. None of us are separate; all of us emergent. Art and science are the data-- the evidence-- of that great theory.
